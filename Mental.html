Mental Health & Suicide Data Mapping

A student-led initiative to collect anonymized data for spatial analysis and mental health research. No personal identifiers are collected.

About This Project

This website is designed to support academic research in mental health and suicide prevention. The collected data can be used for geographic mapping, trend analysis, and evidence-based policy recommendations.

⚠️ If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or a trusted mental health professional.
